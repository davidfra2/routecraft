Welcome to RouteCraft

Explore our personalized routes in different cities.

1.1 Purpose

RouteCraft is a mobile application designed to help tourists discover personalized routes in Medellin, Bogota, and Barranquilla based on their location and interests.

1.2 Product Features

  • Real-time optimized itinerary creation.
  • Integration with geolocation systems.
  • Recommendations based on traffic and weather conditions.
  • Adaptable interface for different types of travelers.
  • User reviews and feedback.
  • User calendar generation.
  • Estimated visit time alerts.
  • Route history and trip-based suggestions.

1.3 Product Perspective

RouteCraft is a standalone application integrated with external mapping and traffic services.

1.4 Limitations

  • Dependence on internet connectivity.
  • Accuracy limited by available traffic data.
